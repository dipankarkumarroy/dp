Summary of Key MPI Functions Used Across Problems:
MPI_Init: Initialize MPI environment

MPI_Finalize: Terminate MPI environment

MPI_Comm_rank: Get process rank (ID)

MPI_Comm_size: Get total number of processes

MPI_Send: Send data to specific process

MPI_Recv: Receive data from specific process

MPI_Bcast: Broadcast data to all processes

MPI_Scatter: Distribute chunks of data to processes

MPI_Gather: Collect data from all processes

MPI_Reduce: Perform reduction operation (sum, max, min, etc.)

MPI_Barrier: Synchronize all processes

MPI_Wtime: Get current time for performance measurement
